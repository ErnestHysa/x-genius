# Privacy Policy for X-Genius

We respect your privacy.

*   **Authentication:** We use your X account solely for authentication purposes via Supabase.
*   **Email Address:** We request your email address from X as it is required by our authentication provider (Supabase) to uniquely identify your account.
*   **Data Usage:** Your email and X profile information are used only to create and manage your session. We do not sell, share, or use your personal data for marketing or any other purpose.
